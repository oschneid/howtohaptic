Chapter 1 Introduction

Welcome to howtohaptic! This is a living handbook for designers, researchers, and hapticians of all flavours to quickly reference material related to haptic experience design.

This handbook is currently under development. If you have any questions or concerns, please contact the author, Oliver Schneider.

1.3 How to use this book

  • a short handbook for designers, researchers, and hapticians of all kinds

1.4 Outline of chapters

  1. Introduction tells you how to use this book
  2. Why Haptic? (Chapter 2) tells you what the value of haptics is - why is it important to know how to design for it
  3. What is Haptic Sense? (Chapter 3) gives a brief overview of the sense of touch, with links to references
  4. What is Haptic Tech? (Chapter 4) gives a brief overview of the wide array of haptic technologies, organized into the common groupings
  5. How to Haptic (Chapter 5)gives a primer on designing for haptic senses and technologies.
  6. How to Multisensory (Chapter 6) expands the discussion to multisensory design - how to work with vision, audio, and olfactory feedback.
  7. Example Case Studies (Chapter 7) gives a few detailed examples of haptic experience design, the decisions that hapticians made, and the outcome, to walk readers through the process.
  8. Common Applications (Chapter 8) lists several common goals and application areas of haptics, with their current status and references to essential literature.
